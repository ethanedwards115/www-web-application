How is our store Different?

Here at the little Green Larder we work a little differently from shops you might be used to. In an effort to reduce our waste as much as we possibly can, we operate generally on a bagless system, where customers are able to bring in their own containers and use those to directly store and buy our products with. This is a short guide to help you get used to the concept, and make sure that your next visit is as smooth as possible.

Put as simply as possible, you bring your own little bags or containers, you put what you'd like into them from our delicious products, then you weigh it and pay for it, simple right?

A simple way to visualise our system if you've never used it before is to think of it as a healthier form of the classic pick and mix. The first step is probably the hardest, picking what you want!
pick from any of our great products, but be sure to keep it to one product per container, as they are slightly different prices

Now that you have your bag/container full of whatever you want, you have to weigh it, as this is how the total cost is calculated.
Simply go over to our scale, located right next to the treats in the corner and plop your produce right down. The weight and price will be displayed for you, along with a little recipt.
Finally just take your recipt over to our friendly cashier and pay up, and then that's it, you're free to enjoy your new snacks or spices.
